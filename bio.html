Biography

Two long lost friends meet by chance on a dark and cold suburban night somewhere in Stockholm, set the scene a few years back. They chat while intoxicating themselves, indulging in one of those ”what if” conversations that so eagerly takes place after a few of something, whatever. Realising they both have been either kicked out or left behind by all their ”lets start a band and take over the world” mates they vow never to start any more rock and roll bands.

Next morning they start writing songs together, ”just for fun”. Skip a few years and the two friends carry on in their respective careers while honing their mutual songwriting, advancing into recording and arranging, ”just for fun”. Another year pass and by chance, on another one of those dark and cold suburban nights somewhere in Stockholm, two friends happen to intoxicate themselves on the same place as two other misled musicians. One - a former punk rock drummer turned klezmer-for-hire drummer (later kicked out and resorted to playing in a seedy Södermalm Bar close to Södra Station). The other - child prodigy turned ”famous Swedish black and death metal” star.

Well you get the jitz - the two friends (who by themselves were not exactly what you’d call #marketable) and the two other misfits started a band, kinda. But to get around their promise never to be in bands, they chose Mona to front the operation (also in part due to her capability to send out the forms necessary to get royalties and other stuff). Also she got to name the operation being the best singer and the only one who feels comfortable playing a guitar heavy enough to carry the extensive guitar work.
